Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Monday 25 January, 2047 train services were scheduled to operate of which 1993 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 85% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

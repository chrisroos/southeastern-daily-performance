Saturday 12 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Saturday 12 March, 1706 services were scheduled to operate of which 1694 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time; however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 97.7% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

Please note this information is intended as a guide to the cause of disruption only, and does not necessarily include all trains affected.

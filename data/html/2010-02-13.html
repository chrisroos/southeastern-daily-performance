Saturday 13 February

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Saturday 13 February 1728 train services were scheduled to operate of which 1724 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 97% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

The following services were diverted, cancelled or delayed by over 10 mins:

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Friday 5th March 2059 services were scheduled to operate of which 1931 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day  71% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

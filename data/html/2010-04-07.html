Wednesday 7 April

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Wednesday 7 March 2054 services were scheduled to operate of which 2015 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 92% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

Sunday 28 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Sunday 28 March 935 services were scheduled to operate of which 934 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 98% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

The following services were diverted, cancelled or delayed by over 10 mins: 

Track circuit failure
16:23 Charing Cross - Hastings delayed by 42 minutes

Train fault at Charing Cross
17:35 Charing Cross - Hayes delayed by 11 minutes

Ambulance requested
19:38 Victoria - Ramsgate delayed by 12 minutes

Driver error
19:40 Charing Cross - Gillingham did not call at Abbey Wood

Passenger disorder at Dartford
22:15 Gillingham - Charing Cross delayed by 10 minutes

* Indicates a delay of over 30 minutes

Please note it is possible for a service to be delayed during the journey, but still arrive on time at the final destination.

We are sorry for any inconvenience caused by these service disruptions.

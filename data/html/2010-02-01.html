Daily performance information

Monday 1 February 

 Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Monday 1 February 2050 train services were scheduled to operate of which 1988 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 86% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

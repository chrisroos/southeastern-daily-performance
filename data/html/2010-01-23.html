Saturday 23 January

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Saturday 23 January, 1652 train services were scheduled to operate of which 1596 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 90% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

Monday 29 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Monday 29 March 2047 services were scheduled to operate of which 1979 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 88% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

Saturday 13 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Saturday 13 March 1766 services were scheduled to operate of which 1721 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 95% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

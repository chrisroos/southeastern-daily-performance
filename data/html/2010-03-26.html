Friday 26 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Friday 26 March 2058 services were scheduled to operate of which 2027 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 91% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

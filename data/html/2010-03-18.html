Thursday 18 March

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Thursday 18 March 2051 services were scheduled to operate of which 2033 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 94% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.

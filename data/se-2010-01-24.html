Sunday 24 January

Whether you're one of our regular commuters, travelling on business or for leisure, you expect Southeastern trains to be punctual and reliable.

On Sunday 24 January, 1003 train services were scheduled to operate of which 948 ran.

We aim to run all our trains on time, however there are times when this isn't possible. On this day 89% of services ran within 5 minutes of schedule.
